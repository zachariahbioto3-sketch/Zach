Our Story

Born from the Heart of East Africa

Founded in 2012, Savanna Grill began as a small family dream — to share the bold, vibrant flavours of East African cuisine with the world. Today, we're Nairobi's most celebrated fine dining destination.

Every ingredient is sourced from local farms. Every recipe honours tradition while embracing modern culinary technique. When you dine with us, you taste the land itself.
